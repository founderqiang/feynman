1. Kinome definition

The target gene set is the human protein kinome from the KinHub / Manning classification, enriched with current UniProt gene symbols and NCBI Entrez IDs. After collapsing dual-domain kinases (e.g. JAK1/2, GSK3, OBSCN) to the gene level, the set contains 522 unique kinase genes across 10 groups.

2. Guide selection (Brunello)

Guides were drawn from the Brunello genome-wide human CRISPR-KO library (Doench et al., Nat Biotechnol 2016; 76,441 targeting sgRNAs + 1,000 non-targeting controls, 4 guides/gene, optimized with Azimuth Rule Set 2 on-target and CFD off-target scoring). Mapping the kinome onto Brunello by symbol + curated aliases:

  • 518 / 522 kinases present in Brunello with exactly 4 guides each → 2,072 targeting sgRNAs.
  • 4 absent from Brunello (GRK1, STK38, PRKY, CDK8) → flagged for de-novo design.
  • A false alias match (RP1, retinitis-pigmentosa gene, Entrez 6101) incorrectly mapping to kinase STK19/WHR1 was removed.

3. On-target quality

Every selected guide was screened for GC content, poly-T (TTTT) Pol-III terminators, homopolymer runs, and internal BsmBI/Esp3I cloning sites, alongside its Rule Set 2 score. Brunello guides are pre-optimized, so quality is high: median Rule Set 2 = 0.668, no TTTT terminators, no internal BsmBI sites, GC all within 20–80 %. The only on-target flags are 69 guides with a homopolymer run ≥5 bp and 10 with extreme GC; no gene loses more than 2 guides to on-target flags.

4. Genome-wide off-target prediction

All 2,072 spacers were aligned genome-wide against GRCh38 (Ensembl release 110 primary assembly) with Cas-OFFinder (NGG PAM, ≤3 mismatches), yielding 17,584 candidate off-target sites. Each was scored with the CFD (Doench 2016) and MIT/Hsu specificity metrics, and annotated against Ensembl CDS/exon intervals.

  • Median aggregate MIT specificity = 97 (max 100); the library is highly specific overall.
  • 128 guides (6.2%) flagged high-risk: 114 with a high-CFD (≥0.2) off-target in a coding exon, 13 cutting multiple perfect genomic sites, 3 with very low specificity.
  • All 4 SIK1 guides cut two perfect sites each — these are its near-identical paralog SIK1B (segmental duplication). They still knock out SIK1 and are retained with an annotation.

Reselection logic. Because Brunello supplies exactly 4 fixed guides per gene, flagged guides cannot be swapped within the library; they are retained but annotated (guide_verdict, offtarget_highrisk) so they can be down-weighted in analysis. 37 genes (4 absent + 33 with <3 acceptable guides) are placed on a de-novo worklist totaling 53 custom guides (denovo_design_worklist.csv) for labs wanting ≥3 high-confidence guides per gene.

6. Library construction

Synthesis-ready 83-mer oligos carry inward-facing BsmBI/Esp3I sites with CACCG/AAAC overhangs for Golden-Gate cloning into lentiGuide-Puro (Addgene #52963) or lentiCRISPRv2. The CACCG overhang supplies the U6 +1 G, so spacers need not start with G.

  • Pool is internal-BsmBI-clean: 8 non-targeting controls containing BsmBI and 3 junction-conflict NTCs were removed → 3,129 oligos.
  • 9 targeting guides begin with TCTC, creating a junction BsmBI site; flagged needs_separate_cloning for a parallel BbsI reaction. Each affected gene keeps ≥2 other guides.
  • Cloning representation target ≥300× → ≈939,000 independent colonies; pool QC by NGS (>90–95 % detected, skew <10).

Full step-by-step protocol: construction_protocol.md; oligo sequences: oligo_pool_synthesis.csv / oligo_order.txt.

7. Transduction & sequencing depth

Recommended operating point: MOI ≈ 0.3 (≈26 % of cells transduced, ~1 integration/cell) and ≥500× representation maintained at every passage.

8. Methods & provenance

  • Kinome: KinHub (kinhub.org) Manning classification; symbols/Entrez via UniProt REST.
  • Guides: Broad GPP Brunello library contents (Doench et al. 2016).
  • Off-target search: Cas-OFFinder v2.4.1 (Bae et al. 2014), GRCh38 Ensembl r110, NGG, ≤3 mm, CPU/POCL.
  • Scoring: CFD (Doench et al. 2016) and MIT/Hsu (Hsu et al. 2013) matrices from the CRISPOR reference implementation.
  • Annotation: Ensembl r110 GTF (CDS + exon intervals).

Assumptions: human, SpCas9/NGG PAM, 4 guides/gene, MOI 0.3. Adjust representation and reads/guide via the depth-plan scenarios.
